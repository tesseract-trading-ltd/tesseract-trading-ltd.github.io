A tesseract — the four-dimensional cube — projects its full structure into the three dimensions we can see, and from that projection we trade. Price is one axis. Liquidity, time and correlation are the other three.

Tesseract 是阿莱夫行会的高维度执行分部。我们不在单一价格走带上找超额。超立方体——那四维的方体——会把它完整的结构投影到我们可见的三维，我们便依此投影而行。价格只是其中一轴。流动性、时间、相关性是其余三轴。

ISection章

A trading house, in four axes.

一座四轴交易厅。

No screens of screens. No predictions issued for show. The house exists to act on structure that the price tape alone cannot describe.

没有套娃式的屏幕，也不为表演而发预测。本厅之设，正是为了对那些价格走带本身无从描述的结构作出反应。

A tesseract is not the figure on the page. It is what the page can only ever shadow.

超立方体并非纸上的那张图——它是那张图所能做到的，至多不过是它的影子。

The tesseract — a four-dimensional cube — appears on paper as one cube nested inside another, joined edge to edge. That picture is not the object itself; it is the shadow it casts when it crosses our three-dimensional sight. The Aleph the Guild is named for behaves the same way: a structure too large to be seen, only ever observed in projection. Trading, for us, is the act of reading those projections and taking position before the projection collapses.

超立方体——那四维的方体——画在纸上呈现为一个方体嵌于另一个方体之中，棱棱相连。那张图并不是它本身，它只是它穿越我们三维视野时投下的阴影。行会得名所自的"阿莱夫"亦是如此：它过于宏大，无法被直接看见，只能在投影中被察觉。所谓交易，于我们而言，便是阅读这些投影，并在投影崩塌之前就位的行为。

// From the trading-floor log // 交易厅日志摘录

Where Alchymia Labs distils the alpha, Tesseract executes against it. We are not researchers; we are the hand. Every channel we operate carries the signed filing of the apparatus that bore it, and every fill is reconciled — slippage, queue priority, venue latency — back to the projection that authorised the trade.

Alchymia 实验室提炼阿尔法，Tesseract 则据之执行。我们不是研究者，是出手的那只手。我们所运行的每条通道，都附带产出它的器物的签署归档；每一笔成交——滑点、队列优先级、场所延迟——都须回溯归因至授权此笔的投影。

We are not a fund. We are not a signal vendor. We are the disciplined hand the Guild keeps under glass — quiet most of the day, exact when the projection resolves.

我们不是基金。我们不是信号供应商。我们是行会留于玻璃匣中的那只受约束的手——多数时候静默，惟当投影显形之时，方才精确。

PRINCIPLE · I◇

Project before acting.

先投影，后行动。

A position is only taken once the four-axis projection agrees. If three axes carry the trade and the fourth refuses, we wait — or we do not trade.

惟当四轴投影一致认同时，方才建仓。若三轴支持而第四轴拒绝，则等待——或干脆不交易。

PRINCIPLE · II⬡

Geometry over rumour.

几何，胜于传闻。

Price moves on news, but news is rarely the cause. The cause is the geometry that already permitted the move. We trade the cause.

价格因新闻而动，而新闻鲜少是原因——原因是那早已默许此动作的几何。我们交易原因。

PRINCIPLE · III⌖

Reconcile every fill.

每笔成交，皆须归档。

An execution that cannot be traced back to the projection that authorised it is not an execution — it is a story. Stories cost capital.

无法回溯至授权它的那道投影的成交，不是执行——是故事。故事会消耗资本。

IISection章

Four axes, one projection.

四轴，同一投影。

Each axis carries its own folio, its own filing code, its own signed instruments. None of them moves the house alone.

每一轴皆有自己的卷宗、归档号、署名工具。无一轴可独自驱动本厅。
